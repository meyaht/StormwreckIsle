## Adventure Overview

A nautical introductory adventure starting in **Neverwinter** and sailing to **Stormwreck Isle**, a remote island caught between the territorial conflict of two dragons:

- **Sparkrender** — a blue dragon, destructive, lightning-wielding
- A **bronze dragon** — older, tied to the island's history

The party begins at level 1 and advances to level 3 by the end of the adventure. Themes include exploration, undead, dragon lore, and faction conflict.

Opening encounters:
- **Voyage to Stormwreck Isle** — survive a stormy sea crossing from Neverwinter
- **Drowned Sailors** — first encounter on or near the island involving undead

## Party Dynamics

- **Frontline:** Maren (Fighter) — primary melee tank
- **Skirmisher:** Elldeebee (Rogue) — flanking, high single-target damage, skills
- **Support/Healing:** Tobias (Cleric) — healing, buffs, divine damage
- **Arcane:** Chjork (Sorcerer) — area control, fire damage, chaos

**Coverage gaps:** Low Wisdom party-wide (poor perception and insight as a group). Elldeebee's Passive Perception 12 is the best in the party. Watch for ambushes.

**Synergies:** Elldeebee triggers Sneak Attack easily with Maren in melee. Chjork's Sleep is devastating against groups of weak enemies at level 1. Tobias keeps everyone alive long enough for it to matter.
