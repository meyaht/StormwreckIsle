# Session 1
**Date:** 2026-03-17
**Adventure:** Voyage to Stormwreck Isle / Drowned Sailors

## Summary
The party sailed from Neverwinter aboard the *Compass Rose* under Captain Tarak. A storm hit on approach to Stormwreck Isle and four drowned sailors (undead) boarded the ship from the port side. The party fought them off, arrived at Dragon's Rest, met the monk Varnoth, and accepted a quest to investigate the Cursed Tide sea cave to the east.

## Events
- Party hired as escort by Captain Tarak (half-elf, glass eye, distrusts magic); destination Dragon's Rest, package delivery
- Storm hit on approach; four drowned sailors climbed the port hull
- Party fought off the undead; Tobias's Turn Undead froze all four, turning the tide
- Captain Tarak: "Get them off my ship." Party disembarked at Dragon's Rest dock
- Met Varnoth — monk, silver dragon pendant, keeper of Sharruth's memory
- Varnoth revealed the dragon pendant's significance: Dragon's Rest honors Sharruth, a bronze dragon who died on the island; blue dragon Sparkrender has been encroaching
- Varnoth asked the party to investigate the Cursed Tide cave — source of the undead; two missing acolytes went there a week ago and haven't returned
- LDB negotiated: party gets shelter, meals, and anything found in Sharruth's ruins
- Chjork accepted by casting Sleep on himself and passing out on the floor
- Session ended with party resting at Dragon's Rest

## Combat
**Encounter: Drowned Sailors (4) — aboard the Compass Rose**

Initiative order: Elldeebee (20), Chjork (17), Maren (14), Sailors (12), Tobias (8)

- Elldeebee missed twice with hand crossbow (rolled 6, rolled 8 to hit)
- Chjork hit with Fire Bolt (20 to hit, 5 damage); breath weapon caught 3 sailors (2 damage, only 1 half-damage save passed); Fire Bolt finished the last sailor on deck (23 to hit, 5 damage)
- Maren dropped one sailor with sword (18 to hit, 7 damage)
- Tobias turned all four undead (all failed DC 13 WIS save); then maced one down (19 to hit, 6 damage)
- Elldeebee finished one with rapier + Sneak Attack (18 to hit, 12 damage)
- All four sailors defeated; no party damage taken

## NPC Notes
- **Captain Tarak** — half-elf, glass eye, runs the *Compass Rose*. Dislikes magic. Tolerates the party exactly as much as she has to. Did not stay on the island.
- **Varnoth** — monk, keeper of Dragon's Rest. Calm, measured, watches people carefully. Genuinely cares about the island and his missing acolytes. Not above leveraging the party's situation (storm, no passage home) to get help.
- **Missing acolytes** — two, names unknown, went to the Cursed Tide cave one week ago. Status unknown.
