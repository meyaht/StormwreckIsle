# Session 2
**Date:** 2026-03-18
**Adventure:** The Cursed Tide / The Lighthouse

## Summary
The party investigated the Cursed Tide sea cave, defeated two giant crabs, and destroyed a corrupted bone-and-seaweed knot that was raising undead from the tidal shrine of Sharruth. Rescued two of Varnoth's acolytes. Returned to Dragon's Rest, then headed inland to investigate the lighthouse — where they met Alagaros, an ancient grey-blue dragon who has lived on the island for centuries. Accepted the mission to explore Sharruth's ruins before Sparkrender can get to whatever is inside. Took a long rest at Dragon's Rest.

## Events
- Party entered Cursed Tide cave; found two unconscious acolytes on a ledge in the main chamber
- Defeated two giant crabs (large and small); Chjork used Mold Earth to create difficult terrain under the small one; Minor Illusion mirror distracted the small crab during combat
- Destroyed the corrupted knot in the inner chamber — bone/seaweed mass wrapped around an ancient bronze dragon statue (remnant of Sharruth); Tobias's Turn Undead caused it to recoil; party finished it off
- Green light extinguished; cold lifted; acolytes regained consciousness as the corruption died
- Returned acolytes to Dragon's Rest; Varnoth visibly relieved
- Party chose the inland lighthouse as next destination
- Chjork knocked on the lighthouse door; Alagaros answered
- **Alagaros** — ancient grey-blue dragon, scarred on left flank by Sparkrender 40 years ago; has lived on the island since before recorded history; knew Sharruth personally; lit the lighthouse himself on return from a long absence
- Alagaros confirmed: the tidal corruption was deliberately placed by Sparkrender; Sparkrender wants something in Sharruth's ruins and has grown impatient
- Party asked Alagaros to accompany them; he declined (too large for the ruins, won't leave the lighthouse easily, has unspoken reasons for staying)
- Alagaros gave the party one of his own scales — hold it up in genuine need and he will know
- Party returned to Dragon's Rest for a long rest; depart for Sharruth's ruins next session

## Combat
**Encounter 1: Giant Crabs (x2) — Cursed Tide cave**

Initiative: Elldeebee (22), Chjork (17), Maren (13), Crabs (12), Tobias (7), Small Crab (6)

- Elldeebee missed twice (rolled 6, rolled 8 to hit); hit with rapier + Sneak Attack (16 to hit, 12 damage) on small crab
- Chjork: Mold Earth on small crab (difficult terrain); Minor Illusion mirror to distract small crab; Fire Bolt missed (rolled 1); Fire Bolt hit large crab (23 to hit, 5 damage) — killing blow
- Maren: dropped large crab (21 to hit, 9 damage first hit; 20 to hit, 11 damage killing blow on large); finished small crab (22 to hit, 10 damage)
- Tobias: healed Maren mid-combat (7 HP); maced large crab (16 to hit, 5 damage)
- Elldeebee took 3 damage from small crab claw; healed to full by Tobias after combat (7 HP restored)

**Encounter 2: Corrupted Knot — inner chamber**

Initiative: Elldeebee (22), Chjork (17), Knot (15), Maren (13), Tobias (7)

- Elldeebee missed with hand crossbow (rolled 4 to hit); rapier missed (rolled 11 to hit); rapier + Sneak Attack hit (rolled 11 to hit, 12 damage)
- Chjork: Sleep failed (knot not alive enough); Fire Bolt finished it (rolled 9 to hit, 6 damage — killing blow)
- Chjork hit by green lance from knot; CON save DC 13, rolled 13 — just passed
- Maren: 3 slashing damage (sword passes through ineffectively)
- Tobias: Turn Undead caused knot to recoil from holy symbol; party finished it off in recoil state

## Loot
- **Alagaros's Scale** — deep blue-grey dragon scale; hold it up in genuine need and Alagaros will know

## NPC Notes
- **Varnoth** — visibly relieved at acolytes' return; knows of Alagaros ("He is still there. Good.") but didn't elaborate
- **Acolytes (2)** — young woman and young man, Varnoth's order; were knocked unconscious by the corrupted knot; recovering well; young woman thanked Elldeebee personally
- **Alagaros** — ancient grey-blue dragon; lives in the island lighthouse; scarred by Sparkrender 40 years ago (Sharruth intervened and drove Sparkrender off before dying 6 months later); won't leave the lighthouse (reasons unstated); gave party his scale; responds well to Chjork's directness and Elldeebee's practical questions; does not want the scar healed — calls it "a record, not a wound"
