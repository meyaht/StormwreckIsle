# Session 3
**Date:** 2026-03-21
**Adventure:** Sharruth's Ruins / The Resonance Stone / The Plan

## Summary
The party traveled to Sharruth's ruins, encountered three mercenaries looting a stone chest, recovered a draconic resonance stone (keyed to Sharruth's bloodline, paired with a stone Sparkrender has had for 20 years), and interrogated the mercenaries on the cliff path. Returned to Dragon's Rest, consulted Alagaros at the lighthouse, and devised a plan: use the orb to feed Sparkrender false information about a young bronze dragon on the island, luring him in for a confrontation. Alagaros agreed to leave the lighthouse and participate directly. Three-day preparation period begins. Chjork spends the time learning dragon history and his own lineage from Alagaros.

## Events
- Party departed Dragon's Rest after breakfast; Chjork cast Mage Armor
- Arrived at Sharruth's ruins — stone archway, bronze dragon relief above lintel, bronze inlay floors, vaulted main chamber with Sharruth relief above a stone dais, three branching passages
- Met **Sable** — tiefling historian (loosely), waiting outside the ruins alone for two days; asked to join; Elldeebee agreed with the warning "if you step the wrong way, I'll kill you"
- Left passage: found three mercenaries (dwarf + 2 humans) looting a stone chest with a blue-glowing orb inside
- Chjork walked in casually, said okey-dokey, walked back out — party regrouped outside
- Party followed mercenaries west with intent to cut them off at a cliff choke point
- Chjork cast Sleep (rolled 20 pool) — dropped dwarf and trailing human; woman at front stayed up
- Chjork used Mold Earth to create difficult terrain; woman tripped, Maren caught her
- Elldeebee tied all three; interrogated the woman — hired via go-between in Neverwinter, told to find old stone with blue glow and Draconic markings, no direct contact with employer
- Woman recognized Sable — had seen her researching Sharruth/dragon lineage in a Neverwinter library, flagged it to her employer. Sable now potentially known to Sparkrender's network
- Returned to Dragon's Rest with three bound mercenaries and the orb
- Varnoth identified it as a **draconic resonance stone** — communication device keyed to Sharruth's bloodline, made in pairs; active = someone on the other end is listening
- Sable confirmed: paired stone likely held by Sparkrender for ~20 years
- Varnoth questioned mercenaries; released them at dusk with a warning
- Party voted to use the orb rather than destroy it
- Chjork and Sable went to Alagaros; laid out the plan; Alagaros confirmed Sparkrender has the paired stone and has been monitoring the ruins for 20 years
- **The Plan:** stage a conversation near the orb about a young bronze dragon connected to Sharruth's line being present on the island — the one thing Sparkrender cannot ignore; Chjork provides the draconic voice, Alagaros provides the authentic detail
- Alagaros agreed to leave the lighthouse and come to Dragon's Rest — first time in decades
- Varnoth: Dragon's Rest has dormant protections built by Sharruth; 3 days to strengthen them; lightning is Sparkrender's weapon, stone/water/tight terrain are his weaknesses; Sparkrender's left flank is permanently damaged from Sharruth's bite 40 years ago — he cannot dive from the left
- Timeline: orb activated on evening of Day 3; Sparkrender expected within 1 day of hearing it
- Chjork spent the 3-day preparation learning dragon history and brass lineage lore from Alagaros
- Alagaros to Chjork on final evening: "You were not raised without family. You were raised without knowledge of your family. Those are different things."
