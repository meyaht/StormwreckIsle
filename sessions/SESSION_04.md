# Session 4
**Date:** 2026-03-21
**Adventure:** The Bait / The Confrontation / The End of Stormwreck Isle

## Summary
The party executed the plan — Chjork and Alagaros staged a conversation near the resonance stone to lure Sparkrender. Sparkrender arrived at dawn on the eastern shelf. The party fought him, nearly wiped, but held long enough for Alagaros to pin him on his damaged left flank. Sparkrender was driven off, not killed. The resonance stone was destroyed. Alagaros revealed the truth behind his forty years in the lighthouse — grief, not duty. Chjork invited him to come to Neverwinter. He accepted. The entire cast — Varnoth, both acolytes, Sable, Maren, Tobias, and Alagaros — agreed to travel to Neverwinter together. End of Dragons of Stormwreck Isle.

## Events
- Party leveled up to Level 2 before the confrontation
- Varnoth provided 4 healing potions, a healer's kit, and a vial of Oil of Sharruth's Blessing (applied to Maren's sword — divine charge on first hit)
- Chjork cast Expeditious Retreat (Extended Spell, 1 sorcery point) before combat
- Staged conversation at the orb: Chjork opened ("he's finally ready to fly for the first time tomorrow"), Alagaros provided draconic detail and emotional weight ("Sharruth would have wanted to see this")
- Orb covered; party moved to eastern shelf below Sharruth's ruins before dawn
- Sparkrender arrived at dawn — enormous, blue-black, lightning crackling, damaged left flank visible
- Combat: Elldeebee hit the damaged left flank (rapier + Sneak Attack, 11 damage), Cunning Action disengaged; Maren hit twice with Action Surge (Oil of Sharruth's Blessing flared on contact, 15 damage total) — Sparkrender bloodied fast
- Sparkrender's lightning breath (DC 16 DEX save, 4d10 = 23 damage) — Elldeebee and Maren both failed, both dropped unconscious; Tobias barely survived (passed save, took 11)
- Alagaros dropped from the cliff, pinned Sparkrender from above-right, forced damaged left flank to the ground
- Tobias stabilized Elldeebee (Spare the Dying); Chjork sprinted to Tobias, fed him a potion (7 HP), dragged him to Maren via Expeditious Retreat dash; Tobias healed Maren (8 HP); Maren stood up
- Chjork used Mold Earth under Sparkrender — destabilized his footing, Alagaros drove him fully down
- Alagaros pinned Sparkrender, threatened his throat, gave him one chance to leave
- Sparkrender recognized Chjork's role: "You tricked me." Chjork: "I was just doing what feels right."
- Sparkrender noted he heard the lighthouse conversation — knew "one foot following the next"
- Alagaros: "It was my plan." Released Sparkrender.
- Sparkrender departed east over the water, bleeding, damaged
- Tobias healed Elldeebee with a potion (9 HP); used second potion on himself
- Orb destroyed — Alagaros crushed it in the courtyard; blue light bled out and dispersed
- Varnoth offered Dragon's Rest permanently to the party
- Alagaros revealed the truth about staying in the lighthouse: not duty, grief. Waiting for something he couldn't name. "It was grief."
- Chjork invited Alagaros to come to Neverwinter and take his life back fully — Alagaros accepted ("Give me a day to put things in order")
- Alagaros will fly ahead; party takes Captain Tarak's ship in two weeks
- Chjork invited everyone: Varnoth, both acolytes, Sable, Maren, Tobias — all accepted
- Campaign end: Dragons of Stormwreck Isle complete

## Combat
**Encounter: Sparkrender — Eastern Shelf**

Initiative: Elldeebee (19), Maren (17), Sparkrender (15), Tobias (11), Sable (8), Chjork (4)

- Elldeebee: rapier to damaged left flank, 15 to hit, 11 damage (1d8+3 + 1d6 Sneak Attack); Cunning Action Disengage
- Maren: Action Surge, two hits — 19 to hit (9 damage, Oil of Sharruth's Blessing flared), 14 to hit (6 damage); Sparkrender bloodied
- Sparkrender: Lightning breath, 90ft line, DC 16 DEX save, 23 damage — Elldeebee failed (down), Maren failed (down), Tobias passed (11 damage, barely conscious)
- Alagaros: dropped from cliff, pinned Sparkrender from upper-right, forced damaged left flank to ground
- Chjork: Expeditious Retreat dash to Tobias → potion → dragged Tobias to Maren; Mold Earth under Sparkrender (destabilized footing, decisive)
- Tobias: Spare the Dying on Elldeebee; healed Maren (8 HP); healed self with potion; healed Elldeebee with potion (9 HP)
- Sparkrender driven off; not killed
